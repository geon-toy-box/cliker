■ Keyboard ASMR at your fingertips

cliker is a dead-simple clicker: press an on-screen mechanical keycap and unwind

Pick the feel you like. Each switch has its own press/release sound and haptic

· Ripple — waves of light spread from where you press
· Solid — a steady glow in your chosen color
· RGB cycle — the color slowly rotates through the rainbow
· Reactive — a bright flare on each press that fades out

■ Stats that add up

See how much you've clicked at a glance.
· Lifetime — total clicks since you first started
· Session — clicks since this launch
· CPM — clicks per minute (your current pace)
· Best CPM — your all-time record

cliker collects and transmits no personal data whatsoever. Your click counts and
settings are stored only on your device. There is no internet permission, no ads,
no analytics, and no connection to any external server. It works exactly the same
in airplane mode.

· Idle hands during meetings or studying
· Fans of mechanical-keyboard sounds and RGB lighting
· People who want a light app with no data worries

Give it a press. Click.
